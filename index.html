Careers at GlobeIn

Care a lot about something bigger than yourself?

GlobeIn is an early-stage start up with a big vision to disrupt poverty and solve a real global billion-person problem.

We're fighting poverty through job creation and fair wages, one artisan at a time. Through our subscription box and online shop, we delight our mindful customers with crafted, distinct goods and the stories about how our artisans created them.

Our team

Product & Tech

Our product and tech team works across our website, backend, and app, to build the best product for our customers. Located all over the world, we are always looking for new ways to upgrade or simplify the GlobeIn customer experience.

Growth Marketing

Our growth team is filled with driven individuals, ready to take GlobeIn to the next level. Using a data-driven approach mixed with passionate creativity, we look for the best way to tell GlobeIn's story across paid, organic and earned channels.

Customer Support

Our amazing customer support team is full of kind people, based all over the US and the world. We always strive to create the best experience for our customers.

Product

Our product team is responsible for curating the amazing Artisan Boxes you see every month. As well as that, they design and source individual products for the shop and Add-Ons, from all over the world. They are always on the lookout for amazing new artisan groups that we can partner with.

Design

Our design team is responsible for the beautiful designs you see across our website and all of our communications. They collaborate with many of GlobeIn's other team members to work on artisan brochures, promotional materials, and our branding.

Operations

Our operations team focuses on all of the logistics that go into making a subscription box a smooth experience for our customers. From receiving inbound shipments to reaching our customers' doors.
